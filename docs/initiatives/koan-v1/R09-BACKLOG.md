## Rules for creating a child card

Before a child is claimed, that child card records:

1. the user's business sentence and smallest honest C# expression;
2. the exact guarantee and corrective failure associated with that expression;
3. the exact user-visible result;
4. current and target decision owners;
5. the specificity level selected for every shared mechanism;
6. red tests and deletion targets;
7. the smallest affected verification matrix; and
8. stop conditions that prevent a universal abstraction or second source of truth.

No child exists merely to introduce an interface, registry, builder, or base class. The first consumer
and meaningful result ship in the same slice.

The preceding slice names the exact next outcome and restart point; it cannot satisfy the next child's
assessment by proxy.
